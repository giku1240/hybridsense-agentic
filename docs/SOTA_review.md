# State-of-the-Art (SOTA) Review: Personalized Physiological Risk-Aware Agents

This document summarizes the current State-of-the-Art (2024-2025) in Multimodal Sensing, Agentic RAG, Clinical Safety, and JITAI, specifically for mental health applications.

---

## 1. Multimodal Emotion & Stress Detection (Perception)
SOTA has moved from simple classifiers to **Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLMs)** that treat physiological signals as first-class reasoning tokens.

*   **Emotion-LLaMA (NeurIPS 2024):** Integrates audio (HuBERT), visual (MAE), and text. Focuses on "emotion reasoning"—explaining *why* an emotion is felt rather than just labeling it.
*   **EmoLLM (2024):** Uses **Multi-perspective Visual Projection** and **EmoPrompt** to guide LLMs in nuanced emotional reasoning.
*   **Lingo-Aura (2026):** Bridges the gap between low-level physiological intensity and high-level linguistic reasoning using a **Double-MLP Projector** to align signals with LLM embeddings.
*   **Physiological Alignment:** Modern frameworks use **1D CNNs** to map raw BVP, EDA, and ST signals into "soft prompts," allowing LLMs to "calibrate" empathy based on physiological arousal.

## 2. Agentic & Clinical RAG (Retrieval)
The focus has shifted to **cyclic, self-correcting** workflows that ensure medical faithfulness.

*   **Self-RAG (2024):** Uses internal **Reflection Tokens** (`[Retrieve]`, `[IsRel]`, `[IsSup]`, `[IsUse]`) to critique its own retrieval and generation.
*   **Adaptive-RAG (2025):** Employs a **Query Classifier (Router)** to match retrieval effort to query complexity, balancing cost and accuracy.
*   **ClinicalRAG (2024):** A multi-agent pipeline designed specifically to mitigate medical misinformation by cross-validating heterogeneous knowledge sources.
*   **MedRAG & MIRAGE Benchmarks:** Establish that domain-specific embeddings and curated medical corpora (PubMed, textbooks) are essential for clinical safety.

## 3. Clinical Safety Guardrails (Protection)
SOTA involves **layered safety architectures** combining general-purpose filters with clinical nuance.

*   **Llama Guard 3:** SOTA classifier for **MLCommons taxonomy** (Suicide & Self-Harm detection).
*   **NeMo Guardrails (Healthcare Rails):** Programmable middleware using **Colang** to enforce HIPAA compliance, PII redaction, and "No Diagnosis" rules.
*   **MindGuard (2025):** Specialized mental health classifiers that reduce false positives by distinguishing between therapeutic "distressing content" and "actionable risk."
*   **HealthBench / MedSafetyBench:** The gold standard for evaluating LLMs against medical ethics (AMA Principles) and clinical safety.

## 4. Just-In-Time Adaptive Interventions (Action)
Integrating LLMs into **Reinforcement Learning (RL)** to solve the "receptivity" problem.

*   **LLM+TS (Thompson Sampling) Framework:** Traditional RL proposes interventions, while an LLM acts as a **semantic action filter**, ensuring suggestions are contextually appropriate.
*   **Receptivity Modeling:** Using sensors (IMU, location) to detect "interruptibility"—ensuring the agent doesn't intervene during meetings or sleep.
*   **StepCountJITAI:** A NeurIPS simulation environment for training RL policies on synthetic users before real-world deployment.
*   **AddHRVr:** A breakthrough in using **Non-Metabolic HRV** (reductions not caused by movement) as a high-precision trigger for stress interventions.

## 5. Bio-Digital Twins (Architecture)
*   **Biopsychosocial Digital Twins (BADT):** Merging genetic, physiological, and environmental data into a single, evolving system.
*   **Cognitive-Physiological Twins (CPDT):** Simulating long-term cognitive and emotional evolution for predictive psychiatry.
*   **Edge-Cloud Synergy:** On-device processing for real-time crisis detection, cloud for heavy-duty trajectory simulation.
